Q: Will it work with my application.

A: Most document-based application are supported out of the box. You can just browse to a file you want to bookmark. Other applications may require plugin.

Q: Can I share bookmarks between different Mac

A: Yes, you can share bookmarks by using shared drive (e.g. iCloud documents or DropBox). Other mac can subscribe to those files.
