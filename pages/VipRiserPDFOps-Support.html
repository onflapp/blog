Q: the rendered HTML page doesn’t look exactly like the original PDF

A: This is expected, unfortunately. The PDF is very different from HTML in many ways. VipRiserPDF Ops try’s to do the best it can (and it should be said the result is good in most situations). The biggest problems is usually the fonts. The VipRiserPDF Ops will have to substitute your embedded fonts with standard webfonts and this may have an impact on the layout of the page. You’ll get the best result if you use web-save fonts only when creating the PDF.
