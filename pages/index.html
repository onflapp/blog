Make it work for you

All OnFl Apps are designed to be customizable and extendable from the start. You can “glue” them together with other apps, creating complex workflows to simplify the way you work.

Each app has its own particular purpose and is intended to enhance functionality of your existing apps as much as possible, rather than completely replace them.
